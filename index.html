Best Places to Eat Outside of Staten Island Tech

Chipotle

Order Chipotle!

Chipotle tastes really tasty, it it relatively cheap compared to going to a resturant, you can get unlimited rice, beans, and dressings for your bowl. You can also a get a free water cup and fill it with soda. So overall Chipotle is the best location to grab a bite outside of SITHS.

ShopRite

Shoprite Order

ShopRite is a great place to eat after school with many options like pizza, sushi and chinese. Overall it is a really good spot.

Carvel

Ice Cream!

Carvel is a great spot on a hot summer day and if you and your friends just want a quick bite to eat. After eating carvels you will feel full and refreshed on some good ice cream.
